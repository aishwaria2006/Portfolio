What is SmartCert?

SmartCert automates certificate requests using Google Forms, Sheets, and AI-powered workflows. It integrates with Python, UiPath, Twilio, and Gemini API to make certificate approvals instant and paperless.

Key Features

Automated Approval

Auto-verifies form entries and routes them to the Principal in one click.

Notifications

Email + SMS alerts sent at every stage using Twilio and Gmail.

Digital Signature

Applies e-signature and image stamps on certificates automatically.

How It Works

1

Student submits via Google Form

2

Sheet auto-updates with entry

3

Python/UiPath processes request

4

Certificate signed & sent via email
